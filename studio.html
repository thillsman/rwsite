No experience required.

But you don't have a computer science degree, you way? No need! RareWire's easy-to-learn language was designed with you in mind. The WIRE language looks like HTML but gives you true native power.

Code from anywhere.

Most app development platforms require you to download bulky software to write and build your apps. Our cloud-based system does the heavy lifting for you. Just write your code in your web browser, and we'll save the files on our end.

iOS. Android. No fighting.

You can do both! When you write your code in our WIRE language, you're one click away from publishing for both of the two major mobile operating systems. Keep your code the same, or custom tweak the details by platform.

Penny-pinchers welcome.

Learning, building, and testing your app will always be free. In fact, we don't even ask you for your credit card when you sign up! When it's time to publish your app, you have the option to pay per publish or buy a subscription. Both are way less expensive than hiring a developer to build your app for you.

Instant gratification is the norm.

So you're working on your code and you want to see your app? With Fusebox, our on-device testing app, you can see the output of your code nearly instantaneously.

We play nice with others.

Does your app need to connect to a web service or API? We've integrated common APIs like Facebook, Twitter, Flurry, and more directly into our app engine. We've also made it easy to add any REST API service, opening up the world to your app.
